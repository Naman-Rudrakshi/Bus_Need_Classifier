Bus Need Project - SAAS DF FA25

About the Bus Need Classifier Project

This was my first personal ML project, completed as part of SAAS DF FA25. The goal is to classify students and neighborhoods by their likelihood of needing a school bus, helping districts design bus routes that best meet student transportation needs. Data: 2017 National Household Travel Survey, U.S. Department of Transportation. Model: Random Forest Classifier. Metrics: See below for detailed model performance. Thank you to Brandon, Arnav, Avery, Sona, and the rest of DF for supporting this project.
